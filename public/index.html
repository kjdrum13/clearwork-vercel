The honest part

What Clearwork is — and isn't.

Clearwork uses AI to synthesize your answers into personalized plans and feedback. It's not a chatbot. It won't remember you between sessions yet. It won't replace a real coach, a real business partner, or a real conversation with someone who knows you.

What it will do is help you get clear, make a plan, and stay moving — on your own timeline, without judgment, without an audience, and without the pressure of performing progress for anyone else.

It's a private workspace for one project at a time. That's exactly what it's supposed to be.
